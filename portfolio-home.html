My Biggest Project

During my Final Year Project, I decided to explore an area that had always sparked my curiosity: computer vision integrated with physical automation.
The project's goal was to develop a system capable of detecting objects or movements through a camera, using Python and libraries like OpenCV, and sending commands to an Arduino, which would perform physical actions based on the detections such as activating a motor for example.
I chose this topic both because of the relevance of computer vision in various fields (industry, security, medicine) and because of the technical challenge it represents. The integration of software and hardware required extensive research and practical testing.
